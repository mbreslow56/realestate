  • Magnificent contemporary design and exceptional architecture villa, located on top of a hill overlooking the entire south coast of Ibiza, with spectacular sea views.
    On an plot of 40,000 m2 that guarantee maximum privacy, the villa enjoys a privileged location just 10 minutes drive from the city, and is ideal for those seeking the privacy of a private villa without sacrificing the hectic city life .
