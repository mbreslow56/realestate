  • Modern villa of 200m2 located in the area of S'Argamassa between Cala Pada and Cala Martina.
    The villa was built in the year 2016 with modern materials that enhance the quality of its architecture. The plot is about 900m2 and is completely fenced. The decoration is very pleasant for the client since it combines the modern style with the Ibizan style.
    The villa has beautiful views to the Mediterranean Sea and to the island of Formentera, as it is situated on the first line of the sea with direct access from the property.
    There are many beaches around which can be reached by walking such as Cala Martina, Es Niu Blau or Cala Pada. Supermarkets are 5 minutes away by car in Santa Eulària or there is also a local supermarket in the area of S'Argamassa just 2 minutes by car.
    The well-known Nikki Beach Club is about a 10-minute walk away, as well as several well-known restaurants in the Santa Eularia area. The city of Ibiza is about 25 minutes by car and Ibiza airport is about 30 minutes from the villa.
